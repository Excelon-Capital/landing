About Excelon Capital

Excelon Capital is a fund management partnership that employs a comprehensive analytical framework to generate higher than benchmark returns without the hassle of due diligence for our investors. We pride ourselves in our high historical returns.

We started in De La Salle University's Animo Labs startup incubator, when three friends from the School of Economics had a dream. Now, we are a team of 17, and our members' diverse group of skills are the reason behind this fund's exemplary performance since its inception.
